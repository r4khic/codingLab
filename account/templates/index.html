OUR STORY

Our team comes with your experience and knowledge

RateIt is a platform for quickly researching and rating professors. The website allows users to navigate whilst peeking appropriately at both educational institutions and teacher.

The users provide an additional review based on their performance. RateIt will provide a diversity of categories, by which teachers and institutions could be depicted.

Considering that we focus on assessing professors and teachers with different backgrounds, we believe students and institutions would from our service.

Besides, public & private institutions may lead their profiles, which means parents might be interested in searching for the most suitable organization for their children.

We have started our path as three group mates with the similar perception of what the modern institutions lack. Our team used to consist of three people - Yerassyl, Rakhatbek and Alikhan. Unfortunately, Alikhan had to leave the project due to external factors, but it’d be unfair not to mention him, given what he contributed to the progress.

Now one of is dealing with the Front side of the website, while another one is focusing on the Back-end. We’ve known each other for half a year, but face a shortage of time, therefore, we split the workload equally.
